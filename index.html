This particular graph however is of little to no help as it hardly takes into account other factors such as demographic of a country or its area.


For the second graph, we calculate the percentage increase in confirmed cases since the previous day country-wise.



The percentage increase gives better insight on how successful the countries have been in containing the infection.

It is calculated as (Confirmed Cases Today - Confirmed Cases Yesterday)*100/Confirmed Cases Yesterday
